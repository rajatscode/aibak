Gameplay

Three Steps to Victory

Every game is a battle of wits. Deploy your forces, strike at weaknesses, and control the map.

1

Deploy

Place your armies strategically each turn. Every territory you control generates income for reinforcements.

2

Move

Attack enemy territories and reinforce your borders. Deterministic combat means the better plan always wins.

3

Conquer

Control bonuses for extra income. Eliminate your opponent to claim victory and climb the ranked ladder.

What Sets Us Apart

Built for Competitive Play

Every feature is designed to reward skill, create depth, and keep you coming back.

Deterministic Combat

No dice, no luck. Pure strategy. Every battle outcome is determined by army counts and kill rates you can calculate.

Fog of War

Limited visibility creates uncertainty and bluffing opportunities. You only see your territories and their neighbors.

MCTS AI

Train against a Monte Carlo Tree Search opponent that evaluates thousands of possible futures each turn.

Glicko Rated

Skill-based matchmaking with a competitive ladder. Your rating reflects your true strength with confidence intervals.

Map Editor

Create and share custom maps. Design your own territories, bonuses, and connections for endless variety.

Live Win Probability

Watch the odds shift as you play. Real-time analysis powered by Monte Carlo simulation shows who is ahead.
