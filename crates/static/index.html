How to Play strat.club

  1. Pick — Select 4 territories in priority order (ABBA draft with your opponent)
  2. Deploy — Place your income armies on territories you own
  3. Attack — Click source → target to attack adjacent enemies (60% offense / 70% defense kill rates)
  4. Conquer — Capture all enemy territories to win
Bonuses: Control all territories in a region for extra income each turn.
Fog of War: You can only see territories adjacent to yours.
